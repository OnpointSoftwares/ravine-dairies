Ravine Dairies Achieves Carbon Neutral Status

We're proud to announce that Ravine Dairies Cooperative has officially achieved carbon neutral status, making us the first dairy cooperative in the region to reach this milestone.

Through our comprehensive sustainability program, including solar energy installation, methane capture systems, and regenerative farming practices, we've successfully offset our carbon footprint while maintaining the highest quality standards.
