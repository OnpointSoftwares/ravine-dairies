Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We deliver to all areas within 50km of our facility. This includes major cities and surrounding rural areas. You can check if your area is covered during the checkout process on our online store.

All our products are delivered within 24-48 hours of production. Our milk is typically delivered within 24 hours of milking, and our cheese and yogurt products are made fresh daily or weekly depending on the type.

Yes, all our products are certified organic. We maintain strict organic farming standards across all our member farms, ensuring no synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or artificial additives are used.
