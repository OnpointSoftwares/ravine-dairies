Our Story

Founded in 1985 by a group of 12 passionate dairy farmers, Ravine Dairies Cooperative was born from a shared vision of creating a sustainable, farmer-owned business that prioritizes quality, community, and environmental stewardship.

What started as a small collective of local farmers has grown into a thriving cooperative representing over 150 family farms across the region. Our commitment to fair trade practices, sustainable agriculture, and exceptional dairy products has made us a trusted name in the community.

Today, we continue to honor our founding principles while embracing innovation and modern farming techniques that benefit both our farmers and our customers.

We collaborate closely with dairy farmers to bring fresher milk and dairy foods to consumers. Our milk is collected from the abundant catchment areas of the Great Rift Valley, and we focus on keeping our products as close to their natural state as possibleâ€”so families can enjoy quality nutrition every day.

Our Mission

To provide the freshest, highest-quality dairy products while supporting local farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture, and strengthening our rural communities through cooperative ownership and fair trade practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading example of sustainable dairy farming and cooperative business practices, creating a model that other agricultural communities can follow for generations to come.
