Our Journey

Milestones in our cooperative's history

1985 - Foundation

Ravine Dairies Cooperative was founded by 12 local farmers committed to fair trade and quality.

1995 - Organic Certification

Became the first dairy cooperative in the region to achieve organic certification.

2010 - Sustainability Initiative

Launched comprehensive sustainability program including renewable energy and carbon neutrality goals.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched online platform and direct-to-consumer delivery service.

Our Commitment to Sustainability
